Toronto, Ontario Logo

Incorporating Toronto, Ontario’s beautiful maple leaves as inspiration for my logo. After taking time to research the city of Toronto, I took note of two of their most iconic attractions, the Royal Ontario Museum and their Great Lakes. In the final logo, the geometric shapes symbolizes the Royal Ontario Museum, while the horizontal line creates an illusion portraying Ontario’s Great Lakes. With those two key components combined, it creates the beloved maple leaf.

Step 1

After 30+ sketches and iterations of a logo, I came up with 3 digitized drafts.

Step 2

Then I had a class vote on the best logo design and started filling in the colors. I chose the maple leaves signature changing colors with a more vibrant shade to make the logo come to life.

Step 3

Here is the final version on a light background. The hero image above is the logo on a dark background.
